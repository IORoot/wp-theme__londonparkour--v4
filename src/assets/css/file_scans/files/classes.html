Discovery Class
Canada Water
Age: 14+
£10

Returning to one of the core elements of the discipline, the discovery class is designed to nurture the fabled “Parkour Vision” and develop the skill of self-challenge.

Led by coaches Kevin Francomme and Andy Pearson, this class is structured in an alternative way to traditional parkour classes and will focus upon the following aspects:

  1. Investigate the environment at specific locations around London and develop the ability to recognise potential challenges for yourself and others.

  2. Embrace the spirit of challenge and evolve your ability to overcome fear.

  3. Advance your ability to block out external pressures, the urge for destructive self-comparisons and advance the spirit of group collaboration.


Example Class: Find a personal challenge

The discovery class is less to do with conditioning and drilling techniques, but rather, finding a personal challenge that is at your own honest ability level. The coaches are there to help you understand how to challenge yourself correctly and provide feedback on how to take it further without increased risk or danger.

Outdoor Class East
Canada Water
Age: 14+
£10

This is a fun, practical, parkour movement for everyone. Crafted with the true spirit of parkour and refined to be one of the most enjoyable, challenging and rewarding training sessions you will ever participate in.

Outdoor classes are for everyone, even first-timers. They’re structured to allow you to participate in the same tasks, training and challenges as everyone else, no matter your ability level.

Example: Move over a hip-high wall x30 times.

  • First-timer: Learns the fundamental movement ‘step-vault’ and practices that.

  • Intermediate practitioner: Practices a vault that they need to refine.

  • Advanced practitioner: Coach blind-folds them and the use their weakest vault.

As you can see, the training allows every ability level to participate in the same challenge, with the same focus and goal.

Indoor Class
Canada Water
Age: 14+
£10

Unleash your true movement abilities. Learn to move the way your body was built. The beginner indoor classes are the best way to support your training and improve your technical abilities.

Indoor classes are a fantastic way to learn parkour and build upon existing skills. With such a wide array of topics, the classes change focus every week, with new challenges and goals for you to accomplish, but will hinge on specific themes and attributes to you become a better mover.

General class format:

  1. Joint Mobility

  2. Thorough Warmup

  3. Technical work and Skills

  4. Strength training & Body Armour

  5. Challenge

  6. Warm down

  7. Stretching

This is the general “bell-curve” approach of training. This template is the way classes are formatted, which allows for maximum learning and experience across the two hours.

Youth Class
Vauxhall
Age: 8 to 14
£15

Kids that are healthy, fun and smart. Get your children to understand early on how to remain mobile, enjoy movement and become better humans.

In today’s society, we believe that our youth need an honest, enjoyable, professional and disciplined way of learning how to move. At LondonParkour, we give kids real challenges where they learn about the fundamentals of challenge, personal fear and how to approach and tackle it.

General Class goals

  • To provide challenges and achievable goals that promote human growth.

  • Give a continual feedback loop where the constantly grow.

  • Enjoy moving and learn to adapt, explore and experience.

  • Foster collaboration, rather than competition. Helping each other, not dominating.

  • Expand the way they see the world and the possibilities of moving within it.
